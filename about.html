ROTARY JOINTS

Rotary joint also known as rotating joint, rotary union, swivel joint etc. are a system of dynamical sealings,The rotary joint is a mechanical device that allows transfer of working medium from a stationary source (stationary supply pipe) to a rotating machinery (drum, roll or spindle) for cooling, heating or transfer of fluid power.
Rotary joint are used in the following industries: steel industry (continuous casting steel), paper industry, textile industry, rubber industry, plastic industry, food industry), animal waste processing machinery, agriculture and machine tool spindles.

MECHANICAL SEALS

Mechanical seal is a part of process equipment (pump, compressor, mixer…). Basic purpose of mechanical seal is to prevent the leakage of working medium (petrol, acids, water, milk, beer, sugar syrup, oil, bases …) from process equipment to environment.
